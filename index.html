about us

Expert Manufacturers of Precision Machined Components

Beekay Precision has built extensive expertise in manufacturing precision machined components over the years. Located in Pune, we are strategically positioned to serve our customers effectively.

We specialize in:

  • Custom component manufacturing
  • Precision machining
  • High-quality electrical and electronic components

Our emphasis is on providing complete solutions, from design to finished product. Whether you need assistance in designing and creating a prototype, batch production, or volume production, we strive to develop the skills and equipment to help you achieve the highest standard products.

Our Capabilities

Design

Apart from standard items, we fulfill custom requirements. We work closely with OEM engineers and product managers to design parts that fit their specific applications.

Development

We offer complete prototyping services for validation and proper fitment. Our in-house expertise includes tool design and manufacturing of dies, moulds, fixtures, and SPMs for volume production.

Production

We have the capability to produce and supply volumes according to customer schedules, maintaining consistent quality throughout.

Quality

Quality is our foremost promise. Every component manufactured at Beekay Precision undergoes rigorous quality control measures to ensure excellence.
